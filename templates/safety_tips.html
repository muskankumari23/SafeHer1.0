Safety Guidelines & Tips

Read through these essential safety guidelines designed to help you stay aware, prepared, and safe in various situations.

🚕 Travel Safety

  • Always share your live journey details with a trusted contact before boarding a cab.
  • Check the child-lock mechanism on cab doors before the ride starts.
  • Avoid giving out personal information like your exact address to drivers.
  • Keep the SafeHer dashboard open and active when traveling at night.

🌙 Night Commutes

  • Stick to well-lit, populated routes whenever possible.
  • Stay alert—avoid using noise-canceling headphones when walking alone.
  • Walk purposefully and confidently to your destination.
  • If you feel followed, head directly to a crowded place like a 24/7 store or gas station.

📱 Digital Safety

  • Do not check-in on social media until you have left the location.
  • Review your privacy settings on social accounts periodically.
  • Keep emergency contacts updated on your SafeHer profile.
  • Ensure your phone is fully charged before leaving home.
